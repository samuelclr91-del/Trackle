Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

In short: Trackle does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data we collect

Trackle does not collect personal data. All information you enter into the app (subscriptions, prices, categories, user profiles) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

We do not use our own servers, nor third-party servers, to process or store your information.

2. Apple Intelligence

Trackle uses Apple Intelligence to power its financial-analysis assistant. This processing runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device foundation model. No data is sent to external servers for this purpose.

On devices that don't support Apple Intelligence, the assistant falls back to a local rules-based engine — also without any server connection.

3. Purchase data

Subscriptions and in-app purchases are handled exclusively by Apple and the App Store. Trackle has no access to your payment information, credit card, or billing data. See Apple's privacy policy for more details.

4. Anonymous analytics (optional)

Trackle offers the option to share fully anonymous usage data and crash reports. This feature:

  • Is disabled by default
  • Is fully optional and can be turned on or off any time from Settings
  • Does not include personal data, subscription names, or amounts
  • Is used solely to detect bugs and improve the app

5. Third-party services

Trackle uses the following services to display icons for your subscriptions:

  • iTunes Search API (Apple): to fetch app icons
  • DuckDuckGo Icons: to fetch website favicons

These requests are made to look up icons and do not transmit personal data.

6. Children's data

Trackle is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information, please contact us so we can take the necessary action.

7. Security

Because no data is transmitted to or stored on external servers, the risk of a data leak is minimal. Your information is protected by the security features of your own Apple device (Face ID, passcode, disk encryption).

8. Changes to this policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be published on this page with the corresponding update date. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
