Under the hood

Built natively for iOS.

Trackle isn't a port. Every detail is crafted with native Apple frameworks so it feels like part of your iPhone.

Private by design

All your data lives on your device. No accounts, no servers, nothing to leak. Optional iCloud sync stays inside your private Apple account.

Apple Intelligence

The AI assistant runs on-device using Apple's foundation model. On older devices, a smart on-device engine kicks in — always without sending anything off your phone.

Widgets, Siri Shortcuts, Lock Screen and Home Screen integration. Trackle behaves exactly like the apps Apple ships with iOS.
