Features

Everything you need to stay on top of your spending.

From streaming and gym memberships to cloud storage — Trackle keeps every subscription organized, predictable and easy to understand.

Smart reminders

Never get caught off-guard by a renewal. Trackle tells you what's coming and how much it'll cost — days before you're charged.

Spending analytics

Beautiful monthly and yearly charts show exactly where your money goes, broken down by category, currency, or service.

AI assistant

Powered by Apple Intelligence on-device. Ask questions about your spending and get instant insights — no servers, no tracking.

Multi-currency

Mix euros, dollars, pounds, yen — Trackle converts and totals everything cleanly so your dashboard always tells the full story.

Under the hood

Built natively for iOS.

Trackle isn't a port. Every detail is crafted with native Apple frameworks so it feels like part of your iPhone.

Private by design

All your data lives on your device. No accounts, no servers, nothing to leak. Optional iCloud sync stays inside your private Apple account.

Apple Intelligence

The AI assistant runs on-device using Apple's foundation model. On older devices, a smart on-device engine kicks in — always without sending anything off your phone.

Native everywhere

Widgets, Siri Shortcuts, Lock Screen and Home Screen integration. Trackle behaves exactly like the apps Apple ships with iOS.
